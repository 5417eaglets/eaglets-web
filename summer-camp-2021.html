SUMMER CAMP 2021

ABOUT THE CAMP

In this five day online camp, rising 5th and 6th graders will be introduced to basic robotics through custom 3D-printed Arduino robot kits. During the camp, each student will be provided with a robot kit, and students will learn about the components of the kit and how to program a robot to perform various driving maneuvers. Registration for the camp is on a first-come, first-serve basis.

WHY ENROLL?

  • Learn the fundamentals of building robots from knowledgeable instructors
  • Gain hands-on experience with robotics
  • Compete in fun challenges and win prizes!

CAMP DETAILS

  • Dates: Monday, July 26th to Friday, July 30th
  • Times: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM, every day
  • Location: Virtual - Zoom
  • Cost: Free
  • Capacity: 6 students

REQUIREMENTS

  • NO robotics experience needed!
  • MUST be a rising 5th or 6th grader in Allen ISD
  • MUST have a desktop or laptop with a Microsoft Windows or macOS operating system, as well as a USB port

REGISTRATION

  • Registration Window: July 16, 10:00 AM - July 21, 11:59 PM
  • Registration Link: Registration will be through Google Forms. The registration link will be released on July 16th.
  • Registration is on a first-come, first-serve basis. Once registration is full, students will be put on a waitlist.
