BECOME A SPONSOR

Eaglets: Robot Kits for Kids is a non-profit organization run by high school volunteers. Our robot kits are distributed at no cost, meaning that all of our funding comes from our sponsors. One hundred percent of our proceeds from sponsors go toward purchasing necessary materials for constructing our robot kits. Please consider becoming a sponsor to help us expand our program so that our kits can reach more students across the community!
