PROGRAMS

FALL ROBOTICS CAMP 2021

In this camp, 4th through 6th graders will be introduced to basic robotics through custom 3D-printed Arduino robot kits. During the camp, each student will be provided with a robot kit, and students will learn about the components of the kit and how to program a robot to perform various driving maneuvers. Registration for the camp is completed.

Dates: Every Saturday from September 18 to November 11, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Cost: $48

Location: AISD STEAM Center Makerspace

SUMMER ROBOTICS CAMP 2021

In this five day online camp, rising 5th and 6th graders will be introduced to basic robotics through custom 3D-printed Arduino robot kits. During the camp, each student will be provided with a robot kit, and students will learn about the components of the kit and how to program a robot to perform various driving maneuvers. Registration for the camp is on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Dates: July 26 to July 30, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Cost: Free of charge

Location: Virtual Zoom
